Welcome!

Activity Tracker helps you build the habits you want and quit the ones you don't. Track your daily activities with a clean, intuitive calendar view and visualize your progress over time.

How to Use

1. Add Your Activities
Go to the "My Activities" tab and tap the blue + button. Choose whether you want to Build this habit (positive activities like exercise, reading) or Quit this habit (negative activities like smoking, junk food).

2. Track on Calendar
Switch to the "Calendar" tab and tap any date to log your activities. You can track multiple activities per day.

3. Understand Day Colors
• Green days: Only positive habits (build)
• Red days: Only negative habits (quit)
• Amber days: Mix of both positive and negative

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Yes! Your data is encrypted and stored securely using Supabase. We use Google Sign-In for authentication and never share your data with third parties.

Q: Does my data sync across devices?

A: Yes! Sign in with the same Google account on multiple devices and your data will sync automatically.

Q: Can I export my data?

A: Data export feature is coming soon! We're working on allowing you to export your activity history in CSV format.

Q: How do I delete my account?

A: Sign out from the Settings tab. If you want to permanently delete your data, please contact us at the email below and we'll process your request within 48 hours.

Q: Is there a subscription fee?

A: Activity Tracker is currently free to use! We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.
